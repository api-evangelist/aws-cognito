Amazon Cognito introduces an inbound federation Lambda trigger that enables programmatic control over federated authentication flows, addressing challenges such as oversized group attributes and automatic account linking across identity providers. Developers can now intercept and transform federation assertions before they are applied to user pool attributes, improving flexibility in enterprise SSO integrations.
