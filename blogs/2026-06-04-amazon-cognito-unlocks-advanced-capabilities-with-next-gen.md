Amazon Cognito has modernized its underlying infrastructure to enable high-throughput performance, customer-managed encryption keys, and multi-Region replication for user pools. The migration moved hundreds of millions of user profiles with zero downtime, unlocking capabilities that were previously constrained by the prior architecture.
